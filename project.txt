 Roles:

1.	Admin -
Admin has the authority to delete any seller or buyer. Admin can also keep an eye on products and orders placed.


2.	 Seller or Donor -
Seller/donor can add products, with product name, description, quantity, photo, price (sell or donate), location and seller can get the order placed by the user with user details.


3.	Buyer or Receiver -
Buyer or receiver can search the product according to category and place an order. The product will have the description seller name etc.
